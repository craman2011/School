Craig in Verona

 


Verona, Italy.

It is one of the main tourist destinations in northern Italy, because of its artistic heritage and several annual fairs, shows, and operas, such as the lyrical season in the Verona Arena, the ancient amphitheater built by the Romans.

Two of William Shakespeare's plays are set in Verona: Romeo and Juliet and The Two Gentlemen of Verona
